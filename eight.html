    About Freshshop

    Being a grandmother of eight grandchildren seven girls and one boy I realized that there should be a central place where we as parents and grandparents can shop a wide selection of toys and books for the African child. The name African Dream Toys was derived from a simple concept in that all children dream of toys.

    Our vision is to supply African Children with the toys that feeds their imagination and promotes self-esteem while teaching confidence as they see toys that represents the culture that they grow up in.
