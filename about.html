    AFRICAN DREAM TOYS

    Company Histroy

    The company was start by a simple concept; finding a variety of black dolls and action heroes targeted to the black children of Africa. In search of toys for the African child became a challenge. It seems that in the country of Africa, finding toys representing our culture was an impossible task.

    About Us

    Being a grandmother of eight grandchildren seven girls and one boy I realized that there should be a central place where we as parents and grandparents can shop a wide selection of toys and books for the African child. The name African Dream Toys was derived from a simple concept in that all children dream of toys.
